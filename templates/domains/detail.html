YDNS allows it's users to share their domains with others. While this is generally a great thing, we have also seen problems with that.

Some people may stop using the YDNS nameservers for their domains. Once that happens, all YDNS user hosts using those domains will become non-functional. Our platform detects such problems and notifies our staff about that.

In either case we recommend our users to use official YDNS domains, as we never use these domains for anything else than YDNS. Your hosts remain functional for their entire lifetime.
